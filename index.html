About Me
Who I Am

I like data that's a little messy — the kind that comes from real financial systems, real-time event streams, and real enterprise pipelines. Designing it, automating it, and turning it into something a team can actually trust and act on is what I do best.

I'm a Computer Science master's grad from UMass Lowell with 4+ years of hands-on experience as a Data Engineer — giving me a practical, end-to-end view of how data moves: from ingestion and real-time streaming, through transformation and validation, to the dashboard someone's actually looking at on a Monday morning.

Currently building high-performance data solutions at Robinhood, processing multi-terabyte financial datasets with 99.9% platform availability and 99.8% data accuracy.

When I'm not on the clock, I'm usually building something just to see if I can — a fall-detection system using pose estimation, a causal inference model on patient data, anything that lets me dig into a new technique.
